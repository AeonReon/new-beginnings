# Resources Catalogue — Vetted Home-Education Resources for a 4yo

Aligned with classical / Charlotte Mason / Montessori / character-first philosophy. Mainstream public-school-at-home (K12, Time4Learning) excluded.

For each: methodology alignment, age range, price (free/subscription/one-off), what's included, format, parent effort, URL.

## 1. Charlotte Mason Curricula

### Ambleside Online — Year 0
- **Free.** Volunteer-maintained; Year 0 is pre-Year-1 "before-formal-lessons" stage.
- Purist Charlotte Mason — living books, narration, nature study, habit training.
- Birth to ~6.
- Includes: booklists, scheduling notes, parent reading lists, forum.
- Format: web-based text; parents source books from library/used.
- Effort: high prep — you assemble books, plan nature walks, run narration.
- amblesideonline.org

### Simply Charlotte Mason
- Early Years guide ~$15–25; full subject curricula $20–100; some free printables.
- 3–6 and up.
- Lesson plans, Bible, hymn study, picture study portfolios, handicraft guides.
- PDF and print.
- Medium effort — structured but reading-aloud heavy.
- simplycharlottemason.com

### A Gentle Feast
- Petit Feast (3–5 option): ~$45 PDF; printed ~$75.
- Four-year-cycle Charlotte Mason curriculum by Julie Ross.
- Picture books list, nature study, poetry, music, art, hymns, Bible.
- Medium effort — open-and-go-ish with book procurement.
- agentlefeast.com

### Wildwood Curriculum
- **Free.** Secular Charlotte Mason-inspired (a secular counterpart to Ambleside).
- Year 0 for pre-6.
- High effort — similar to Ambleside.
- wildwoodcurriculum.com

### The Alveary (Charlotte Mason Institute)
- Subscription ~$175–225/year.
- Form 0 (pre-6) through upper forms.
- Term-by-term plans, weekly schedules, curated resources, parent training videos, community.
- Medium-high effort.
- charlottemasoninstitute.org/alveary

## 2. Classical Curricula

### Memoria Press — Junior K / Kindergarten
- JrK core ~$200–260; individual books $10–25.
- 4 (JrK), 5 (K). Rigorous classical; Latin-centric trajectory.
- Alphabet book, numbers, classical nursery rhymes, *StoryTime Treasures*, poetry, read-alouds, copybook.
- Physical workbooks + teacher manuals. Low-medium effort — scripted.
- memoriapress.com

### Classical Conversations — Foundations
- ~$400–550/child/year plus registration; curriculum guide ~$60.
- 4–11 (Abecedarians at 4). Community-based classical memory-work co-op.
- Timeline cards, memory work, weekly in-person class.
- Medium effort — community handles structure.
- classicalconversations.com
- **Review carefully** — polarising; visit local chapter before joining.

### Well-Trained Mind Academy
- $400–600/class/year. **Grade 3+ — not for 4yo.** Use *The Well-Trained Mind* book (~$25) for pre-K plan.
- wtmacademy.com

### Classical Academic Press
- Song School Latin is 6+; **mostly not for 4yos**. Flag for later.
- For 4yo: *Bible Songs and Stories*.
- classicalacademicpress.com

### Veritas Press / Logos Press
- Veritas K package ~$150–250.
- Classical Christian publisher. Phonics, Bible, early readers.
- Low-medium effort.
- veritaspress.com, canonpress.com

### CiRCE Institute
- Free podcasts; paid courses $50–300; membership.
- Parent/teacher formation, not a 4yo curriculum.
- Andrew Kern's *Mason Jar*, *Close Reads*, *The Daily Poem* podcasts.
- circeinstitute.org

## Synthesis: One Free + One Paid Pick

### Best free: Ambleside Online Year 0 + Exploring Nature with Children (~$25) + The Good and the Beautiful Pre-K Language Arts (free PDF).

Year 0 at Ambleside is a philosophy and booklist — exactly right for 4. CM argued against formal lessons before six. Ambleside gives the spine (Beatrix Potter, Mother Goose, Winnie-the-Pooh, A.A. Milne, fairy tales) and forum support — free. Exploring Nature with Children bolts on weekly outdoor rhythm. The Good and the Beautiful's free Pre-K Language Arts PDF gives paper-and-pencil touchpoint without pushing early reading. Total: ~$25.

Hits every box for a classical/CM/character-first parent: living books, nature, habits, short lessons, no screens. Effort moderately high — assemble books from library — but the parent's relationship *is* the curriculum at this age.

### Best paid: The Peaceful Press *Preschool Playbook* (~$45), or A Gentle Feast *Petit Feast* (~$45–75).

Peaceful Press Preschool Playbook is the most thoughtfully designed one-volume PDF for a 4yo. Jennifer Pepito — Charlotte Mason-informed, character-led, gentle. Daily flow: read-aloud, simple virtue focus, nature prompt, hand activity, song. Books drawn from the canon (Frog and Toad, Blueberries for Sal, Make Way for Ducklings). Whole day in 45–60 min of gentle time. Assumes no prior teaching experience, generous margin.

A Gentle Feast's Petit Feast: harder CM, fuller structure — hymn study, picture study, composer study, scheduled term. More prescriptive.

### Avoid at 4
Classical Conversations Foundations (memory-work grind mismatch); Well-Trained Mind Academy (too old); Song School Latin (6+); Monti Kids (aging out); any app-first approach.
